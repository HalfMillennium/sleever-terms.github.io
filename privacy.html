Privacy Policy for Sleever

Effective Date: December 30, 2025

Welcome to Sleever (“we,” “our,” or “us”). This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use Sleever, available at https://sleever.space.

1. Information We Collect

Sleever is designed to collect as little personal information as possible.

a. Authentication Information

Sleever uses Google OAuth for authentication. We do not collect, store, or manage passwords.

From Google, we may receive:

  • A basic profile identifier (such as name or email)
  • A unique user ID provided by Google

This information is used only to identify and authenticate you within the app.

b. Spotify Data

If you connect Spotify-related features:

We access public Spotify data only, such as:

  • Public tracks
  • Albums
  • Artists
  • Metadata available publicly via Spotify APIs

We do not access:

  • Private playlists
  • Listening history
  • Account details
  • Personally identifying Spotify data

2. How We Use Information

Information is used solely to:

  • Authenticate users
  • Provide core app functionality
  • Display public music-related data
  • Improve the performance and experience of Sleever

We do not sell, rent, or monetize user data.

3. Data Storage & Security

  • Sleever stores minimal data necessary for app functionality
  • No passwords or sensitive credentials are stored
  • Reasonable technical measures are used to protect stored data

4. Third-Party Services

Sleever relies on:

  • Google OAuth for authentication
  • Spotify APIs for public music data

Use of these services is governed by their respective privacy policies.

5. Cookies & Tracking

Sleever does not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics that profile users.

6. Your Rights

You may:

  • Revoke Google OAuth access at any time via your Google Account
  • Stop using Sleever at any time
  • Request deletion of stored account data by contacting us

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
